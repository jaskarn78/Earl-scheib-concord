About

Same name. Higher standards.

Earl Scheib Of Concord has been under new ownership since 2010 — a small, family-run shop on Market Street that takes pride in real prep and an honest estimate.

A different kind of paint shop.

The Earl Scheib name has been synonymous with honest, quality auto paint for decades. Since 2010, our Concord location has been independently owned and operated — same name, same value-first promise, but with our own commitment to higher-quality materials, proper preparation, and the kind of workmanship you'd want on your own car.

We're a small shop. That means when you bring your car in, you're talking to the people who'll be doing the work. We'll walk you through the right service for your vehicle's age and condition, your budget, and how long you plan to keep the car. No upselling. No phone-quote runaround. Just honest options.

What we believe

Prep is everything.

A great paint job starts long before the spray gun comes out. Sanding, dust removal, chemical clean, seal coat — every job, every time.

Materials matter.

We use the same paints and processes the best dealers and body shops use. Cheaper materials always show up sooner or later.

Honest options.

For minor repairs, we'll discuss self-pay vs insurance — whichever genuinely costs you less.

Treat every car like it's our own.

From a 1970s Caddy to a 2024 daily driver, the standard doesn't change.

Where we work

Our shop is on Market Street in Concord — easy access from Highway 4, I-680, and BART. We serve drivers from Concord, Walnut Creek, Pleasant Hill, Martinez, Pittsburg, Antioch, Pacheco, and Clayton.
